Hello, you too!

I grew up in a small town in the Bale zone, surrounded by farms and fields. My parents were hardworking and instilled in me the values of honesty, integrity, and perseverance. From a young age, I knew that I wanted to make a difference in the world and do something meaningful with my life. After graduating from high school, I attended a small liberal arts college where I studied philosophy and sociology. I was fascinated by the ways in which people think and interact with each other, and I was inspired to pursue a career in social justice. Today, I work developing softwares and in my free time I build websites. It's challenging work, but I find it incredibly rewarding. I'm proud to be making a positive difference in the lives of others.

In my free time, I love to read, write, and listen to music. Reading has always been a passion of mine, and I try to read at least one book a Month. I'm a big fan of literary fiction and non-fiction, and I love discovering new authors and voices. I also enjoy writing, both for my job and for fun. I keep a journal where I write down my thoughts and reflections, and I've also dabbled in creative writing. I've written a few short stories that I'm really proud of, and I'm hoping to write a novel someday.
